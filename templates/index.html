How It Works?

Welcome to the Online Mental Support ChatBot. This chatbot is designed to help you manage and address your feelings in difficult moments. You can type emotions like "sad", "anxious", "stressed", "angry", "fear", "unhappy", "bored", "upset", "stressed", etc. and the chatbot will provide helpful suggestions and resources.

Instructions:

  1. Type an emotion like "sad", "anxious", "nervous", etc.
  2. Receive helpful suggestions or guidance based on the selected emotion.
  3. If unsure, ask for help or start with a greeting like "Hi".

You have the power over your emotions; it's time to help yourself.
